# Receipts And Reports

## Receipt Defaults

Receipts should show:

- SuperSurf or configured receipt heading
- Receipt number
- Subscriber account number
- Subscriber display name
- Payment transaction ID
- Payment channel, such as Paybill or Till
- Amount displayed as KSh
- Amount stored/exported as KES minor units or numeric major units where appropriate
- Business date and time in Africa/Nairobi
- Allocation result, such as renewal, wallet credit, or unmatched resolution
- Configured receipt footer if supplied

Do not display unset public domain, email, Paybill, or Till values. Mark them not configured in settings views.

## Invoice Defaults

Invoices should show:

- SuperSurf or configured invoice heading
- Subscriber account number
- Service plan
- Renewal period
- Amount due in KES
- Wallet applied
- Balance due
- Due or expiry date in Africa/Nairobi
- Configured invoice footer if supplied

## Initial Reports

Reports must use Africa/Nairobi business dates and KES:

- Revenue today
- Revenue this month
- Daily revenue
- Monthly revenue
- Revenue by plan
- Paybill collections
- Till collections
- Unmatched M-PESA payments
- Manually assigned payments
- Reversed payments
- Subscriber growth
- Active subscribers
- Trial subscribers
- Grace subscribers
- Suspended subscribers
- Upcoming expiries
- Wallet balances
- Outstanding renewal charges
- Failed provisioning jobs
- Active sessions
- Data usage from RADIUS accounting where available
- Revenue by service site
- Revenue by access point
- Revenue by county or locality where useful
